: 🌆 Mumbai – The City of Dreams Mumbai, the capital of Maharashtra, is the largest city in India and the country’s financial, commercial, and entertainment hub. Often called the “City of Dreams”, it attracts millions of people who come here to fulfill their ambitions.


. 📜 History & Importance Earlier known as Bombay, it was an important port under Portuguese and later British rule. Today, Mumbai is the financial capital of India, home to the Reserve Bank of India, Bombay Stock Exchange, and major corporate headquarters.

🌟 Famous Attractions Gateway of India – An iconic arch built during the British era. Marine Drive – Known as the “Queen’s Necklace,” a beautiful seaside promenade. Elephanta Caves – UNESCO World Heritage Site with ancient rock-cut temples. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CST) – A Victorian Gothic railway station and UNESCO site. Siddhivinayak Temple & Haji Ali Dargah – Famous religious landmarks. Juhu & Chowpatty Beaches – Popular hangout spots.
