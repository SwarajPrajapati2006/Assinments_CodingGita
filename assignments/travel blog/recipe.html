masala dosa


Masala dosa is a famous South Indian dish made with rice batter and spicy potato filling.

  1. rice batter
  2. potatoes
  3. spices:
    1. mustard leaves
    2. curry leaves
    3. green chillies
    1. oil

    cooking steps:

    1. Prepare the rice batter
    2. Cook the potato filling
    3. Heat the pan and spread the batter.
    4. Add the filling inside.
    5. Roll and serve hot.

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy curry made with paneer, butter, and tomato gravy.

    ingredients

  1. Paneer cubes
  2. Tomatoes
  3. Butter
  4. spices:
    1. garam masala
    2. Chili powder
    3. Coriander powder
  5. cream

cooking steps

  • Fry the paneer lightly.
  • Prepare the tomato gravy.
  • Add butter and spices.
  • Mix in paneer cubes.
  • Garnish with cream and coriander.

    • cooking terms

    Cooking Terms:
    Simmer
    Cooking slowly at low temperature.
    Puree
    Smooth paste made from blending ingredients.
