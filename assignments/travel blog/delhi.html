Delhi

: 🏙️ Delhi – The Heart of India Delhi, the capital city of India, is one of the oldest and most vibrant cities in the world. It is a perfect blend of ancient history and modern development, showcasing India’s cultural, political, and economic importance.


. 📜 Historical Significance Delhi has been the seat of power for many dynasties, including the Mughals and Delhi Sultanate. The city is filled with magnificent monuments, forts, and tombs that reflect its glorious past.


. 🌟 Famous Attractions Red Fort – A UNESCO World Heritage Site and symbol of India’s independence. Qutub Minar – The tallest brick minaret in the world. India Gate – A war memorial and one of the city’s iconic landmarks. Lotus Temple – A Baháʼí house of worship known for its flower-like shape. Akshardham Temple – A modern architectural marvel. Jama Masjid – One of the largest mosques in India. Rashtrapati Bhavan & Parliament House – The center of Indian politics.
